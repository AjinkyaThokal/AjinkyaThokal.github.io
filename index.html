01 / About
Who I Am

Building smarter
with data, every day.

I'm a data professional based in the United Kingdom, currently working as a Data Intelligence Analyst Intern at EssilorLuxottica, one of the world's biggest eyewear companies. I sit right at the intersection of data engineering, analytics and business intelligence, building the pipelines that move data and the dashboards that make it useful.

My background spans healthcare analytics, where I spent two and a half years crunching data for rural health programmes in India, then financial modelling, and now global BI operations and Python automation. I understand the full data stack from raw extraction to stakeholder ready reporting.

Day to day I work with Python, SQL, pandas, xlwings, SAP Business Objects, Power BI, Azure and Excel. I've designed and deployed end to end ETL pipelines that process 145,000+ rows weekly across 5 regional reports, cutting reporting time from over 3 hours down to under 15 minutes. I care deeply about data quality, pipeline reliability and making analysis genuinely actionable.
