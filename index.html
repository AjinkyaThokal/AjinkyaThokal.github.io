Who I Am

Building smarter
with data, every day.

I'm a data analyst based in the United Kingdom, currently working as a Business Intelligence Analyst at EssilorLuxottica. One of the world's biggest eyewear companies. I joined straight out of my MSc in Data Science at the University of Essex, and I've been building real things with real impact ever since.

My background spans healthcare analytics (two and a half years crunching data for rural health programmes in India), financial modelling, and now global BI operations. I know what it's like to work with messy, high-stakes data where the quality of your analysis genuinely matters.

Day to day, I work with Python, R, SQL, Azure, Databricks and Power BI. I'm comfortable right across the data stack, from writing ETL pipelines to presenting findings to non-technical stakeholders.
